At Campus Careers India, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users, including students, job seekers, and partner organizations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Email Address

  • Educational Details
  • Resume / CV
  • Career Preferences

Non-Personal Information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Providing career counseling and guidance

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:
