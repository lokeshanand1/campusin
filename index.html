Frequently asked questions

How long do placements usually take?

Placement timelines vary by profile and market conditions. Most students who actively follow the plan start seeing interview calls within 4–8 weeks.

How long does the study-abroad process take?

From shortlisting to visas, students typically need 6–12 months. We recommend starting at least one application cycle in advance to keep options open.

What is your refund or cancellation policy?

Intro calls are free. For paid packages, we have transparent milestones. If a package is not a fit after the first full session, we offer partial refunds as per our terms.

Who are your counsellors and mentors?

Our team includes alumni from top Indian and global universities with industry experience in technology, consulting, finance, and more.

How do you help with interviews and preparation?

We run profile-based mock interviews, question banks, and feedback sessions so that you are confident across HR, case, and technical rounds.
